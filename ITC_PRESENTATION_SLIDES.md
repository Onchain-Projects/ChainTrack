# SLIDE 5: How It Works

**Headline:** End-to-End Product Journey on Blockchain

### 3-Step Process:

**STEP 1: BATCH CREATION** (Factory)
- Manufacturer creates batch on blockchain
- Unique QR codes generated for each pack
- Merkle root (cryptographic fingerprint) stored on-chain

**STEP 2: MOVEMENT TRACKING** (Distribution)
- Every handover recorded with wallet signatures
- Location & timestamp immutably logged
- Only authorized parties can transfer
